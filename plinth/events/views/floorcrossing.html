FLoor Crossing

How well do you know your mate? Apart from telepathy this game can be won by your writing skills!
In this event, the first participant of a team is given a theme, on which he has to write a story. He would be stopped mid way after a stipulated time and the second participant, who doesnâ€™t know the theme, has to continue it and give it a proper ending.

Problem Statement

Evaluation Criteria

Teams would be evaluated on basis of how close the entire story is to the theme and your creativity.

Format
  1. Event will be conducted on any one of the days from 22 Jan-24 Jan.
  2. Participants are required to come in a team consisting of two members.
  3. The participants will have 3 minutes as preparation time and there are no interjections, no communication among them.
  4. The first participant will start the story for 15 minutes and it will be taken over by second participant.
  5. All the topics will be given on the spot. As soon as first 15 minutes are complete, second participant will take over.

Rules

  1. Exceeding the time limits will result in penalties.
  2. No external help like internet is allowed during the event.
  3. Any decision taken by the organizers will be final and binding on all the participants.

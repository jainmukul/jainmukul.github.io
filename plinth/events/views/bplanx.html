Negotio

Aimed at encouraging people all around looking to fast-track a technology-related business idea or innovation to commercial success.

B-plan competition - Designed to emulate the process of the growth of an idea towards a full- fledged startup. Negotio prides itself on providing a 360 degree, holistic experience in the 1 month period that it spans. Right from acknowledging that your idea has potential, to drafting a B-Model, and pitching in front of an esteemed panel of investor- this platform has it all! Negotio- Serves not only as a launch pad for budding ideas, moved to be recognised as a platform for intensive learning of the entrepreneurial ecosystem by interactions and mentorship from the leaders of this field. With the vision of promoting both business and social innovations, we present.

“ NEGOTIO-THE B-PLAN COMPETITION”

Event Description

Format
  1. Event will be conducted In a big LT on Saturday of the allotted weekend
  2. We are aiming for 200+ startup entries and enthusiastic entrepreneurs from all over the Rajasthan.
  3. Filter out the participating start-ups and arrange the pitching for funding,guidance followed by a networking session.
  4. Registration will be opened on plinth website as follows:
    • Round1 :

      In this Round teams will have to submit a written document describing the nature of the business, the sales and marketing strategy, and the financial background, and containing a projected profit and loss statement in brief.
      The teams will be judged by the mentors of Startup Oasis and the shortlisted teams will then be eligible for 2nd round.

    • Round 2 :

      After filtering and selecting 25 startups from the whole lot within guidance of mentors we’ll send a confirmation mail top these 25 startups requesting their Business model canvas.

    • Round 3: Winners from the second round will be invited to LNMIIT campus for the final pitching round in front of investors , accelerators, incubators.
    • Final round will comprise of presentation and Q-n-A with judges and representatives from funding agencies and incubator organizations. This may be followed by a round requiring the teams to work on an overnight surprise situation.

    • Top two teams will be selected as winners and runners-up and one team from Lnmiit respectively from this round where the three winning teams will be provided with cash prize and many other kind of incentives.

Genral Rules

    Rules
  1. Max-4 candidates can be a part of single team.
  2. Idea will strictly be opened in front of mentors and nowhere else to maintain the confidentiality of an idea.
  3. Team who fail to comply to the deadline of submission of B-plan or confirmation details beforehand would be disqualified.
  4. No startup older than 2-years would be allowed to participate.

Negotio - Live Crowd Funding

Let us to introduce you to "Negotio-The Live Crowd Funding" , the official 'B-Plan competition' cum 'the live crowd funding event' of The LNM Institute of Technology, Jaipur. From recognition of a budding idea to drafting a B-Model to pitching in front of an esteemed panel of investors, Negotio's aim is to emulate the process of the growth of ideas towards a full-fledged startups accompanied by little help through funding made by the live present audience.

Considering the needful requirements of the Minds in spectator ship , there is a promise stated for a 360 degree, holistic experience in the one month period that it spans. Negotio not only serves as a launch pad for budding ideas but is moved to be recognized as a platform for intensive learning of the entrepreneurial ecosystem by interactions and mentor-ship from the leaders of the field of specialization.

Other than the traditional B-Plan competitions , This Crowd-Pitch event deals with an extra cutting-edge section introduced for the sake of a differentiation , freshness and a whole new exciting experience for both the pitchers and the audience. People entering the event premises , are required to pay Rs.200 and take over a "Funding-Voucher". For the purpose of clarity , There is a need to state that this money is not the fees to the event organizers , But this will be wholly provided to each individual's favorite start-up. Thus , they will be presenting their appreciation and feedback through the mode of this "Funding-Voucher".In return they get excellent networking opportunity with some of the well known entrepreneurial minds of India along with a chance to watch the live pitches of some of the best startups of India.

Event Description

Round 1 :

In this Round teams will have to submit a written document describing the problem being solved and a brief summary of the idea . The teams will be judged by the panel of mentors and the shortlisted teams will then be eligible for the 2nd round.

Last day to apply is 5th Jan 2015

Round 2 :

Among'st all the startups entries received top 25 startups would be asked to present a Business model canvas about the idea within 7 days. During this period the top 25 startups will get extensive online mentoring support from elite panel of mentors of IvyCamp & Inspidate so as to furnish and polish their idea to create the perfect canvas.

Round 3:

A total of 10 entries would be shortlisted and these winners from the second round will be invited to LNMIIT campus for the final pitching round in front of investors , accelerators, incubators and idea backers.
Final round will comprise of presentation and Q-n-A with judges and representatives from funding agencies and incubator organizations.
Top two teams will be selected as winners and runners-up respectively from this round and will be provided with cash prize and many other kind of incentives.

Genral Rules

  1. Participants are required to register and submit their questionnaire/ business model online.
  2. A team can have one or more members with a maximum of 4 members in a team. 'Startup Name' cannot be changed over the course of the competition. It should be used for any future correspondence or queries.
  3. A team can submit more than one business model/idea but in doing so, it must submit separately for each submission. The same email-id and/or startup name cannot be used for doing so.
  4. Every contestant must agree with the rules of the contest and must accept the declaration of confidentiality during submission in order to participate.
  5. Team who fail to comply to the deadline of submission of B-plan or further details beforehand would be disqualified.
  6. No startup older than 2-years would be allowed to participate.

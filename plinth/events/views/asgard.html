Asgard

This event consist 2 rounds. 1st round will be online. The participants have to submit a report before the given deadline. After the date of submission, we will select the teams for the second round on the basis of their report. All the teams selected teams will have to present their report in front of the judges, during plinth.

Event Description

Asgard is a newly found planet just like Earth with the following alterations:

  1. Gravity is half of that of earth.
  2. Rotation period of 50 hours and revolution time of 489 days.
  3. Saline water covers 50 percent of the planet.
  4. Existence of no lethal uncontrollable alien species.
  5. Transportation and communication technology have been developed to an extent that we
can reach this planet in three months and any communication signal requires two days to get delivered.

To cater to the needs of our ever increasing population and depleting resources, the human civilisation has to see it's dawn on Asgard, for a comfortable continuation of the species. You have been assigned this tough task to turn into an achievable probability. Describe how this could be done by designing a settlement for 10,000 people. Use your creativity while keeping in mind the above mentioned facts to build upon different aspects of a colony. For example production, distribution and storage of food, management of waste, the problems that would be faced, governing health of people.

Evaluation:
  1. Your report would be evaluated on how many different aspects you have deliberated upon and how many of these are in line with the physics of the planet.
  2. The more descriptive you are, the more points your report will score.
  3. Use can use diagrams and pictures.

Rules

General Rule
  1. Any form of copying of textual material would lead to disqualification.
  2. You can work alone or in a team of at most five.
  3. One project should be submitted by only one team/individual. If same project is submitted by two teams/individuals, first one would be evaluated and second would be disqualified.
Format of the project
  1. The project should be submitted in a pdf.
  2. There is no strict format that you need to stick to. Make it as comprehendible as possible.
Submission :

The final project report should be mailed to astronomy.lnmiit@gmail.com with the subject Plinth: Asgard Project Report: TeamId, From 00:01 hours on 1st November, 2015 and before 23:59 hours on 25th November, 2015.

Startup Intern Fair

Startup intern fair is a one stop place for getting a chance to do an internship in some of the well known and budding startups of the country. Ranging from technical to non-technical domain, one can select the internship of their genre, get screened and get a chance to grab their favorite internship. SIF aims to provide a hassle free and an aggregated hiring process. The time has come to put an end to your impasse and land up in your dream internship..

Event Description

  1. The event is scheduled for Saturday and in the Library lawns.
  2. SIF aims at bringing startups and students under one umbrella.
  3. A form will be rolled out on the Plinth website for the students to register with their details.
  4. Registration fee: 100 (Non-LNMIITians) and free for LNMIITians.
  5. Confirmation mail of the participation along with the schedule of SIF will be sent to all the student participants by 18th January.
  6. Personal interviews shall be done on the spot. It depends on the start-up if they would confirm the hiring on the spot or calls the candidate for another PI round to their office.
  7. Hiring criteria may vary depending upon the startup.
  8. On the spot registrations will be opened/closed depending upon the number of already registered students.
  9. Prior information will be sent to the startups about the number of students and the domain they applied for.

Genral Rules

  1. One candidate can apply for multiple profiles in multiple start-ups
  2. Every candidate should carry atleast three copies of the resume, a passport size photograph and the college ID.
  3. In case of On the spot registrations, printed forms will be available and the candidates will have to fill the forms and sit for the interviews in the respective startups.

Turn Coat

An event where you compete with others by competing with yourself. Given a motion, the participant must support and then oppose it in the course of his/her speech. Can you prove something right, and then prove it wrong then and there, with the same credence? Can you show both the faces of a coin with equal certainity?Come and join this one of a kind debate to find out.

Problem Statement

Format
  1. It is an individual event.
  2. The speakers will have 3 minutes as speaking time.
  3. The switch over between the two parts must be smooth and fluid.
  4. The speaker can choose either English or Hindi as a medium.
  5. The motions will be closed and not open to any interpretation.
  6. All the motions will be given on the spot. Preparation time of 15 minutes will be given.

Rules

  1. Organizer is the boss and the boss is always right.
  2. Exceeding the time limits will result in penalties.
  3. No external help like internet is allowed during the event.

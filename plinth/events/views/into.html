Into The Universe

This event consist of 3 rounds Spacewalk, Sky through your eye and Messier marathon. Detailed description of the event are as follows.

Event Description

1st Round: Spacewalk

Description: It will be an audio-visual quiz related to astronomy. Pictures and videos would be projected in front and participants have to answer the questions that follow.

Format:
  1. Participants will have 1 minute to answer each question by writing it down on the given sheet.
  2. These questions will be objective or one word.
  3. Each question will carry 2 points.
  4. Only top 50% teams will go to the next round.
2nd Round: Sky through your eye

Description: So are you one of those who sneak out of your rooms during night hours and wander long gazing at the sky, trying to build up your imagination. Well folks then this is a perfect game for you to play, a hunt for the stars. Participants need to set themselves on a voyage of different celestial bodies and conquer to best of their capabilities. So, are you ready for this mission to get kicked off? See you at the battle field .

Rules and Regulations

Round 1
  1. Participants can form team of up to three.
  2. Individual participants are also allowed.
  3. Using mobile phones is not allowed.
Round 2
  1. Teams will be provided with certain token numbers and called upon on its basis.
  2. A star map and a laser pointer shall be provided to the team.
  3. Teams are suppose to restrain themselves from using any android application such as Google Sky Map or any other electronic media.
  4. Points will be given for locating the objects precisely only on first attempt.
  5. n-number of teams(where n depends on the participation) would qualify for the final round based on their scores.
Round 3
  1. The teams which had qualified in pointing out the astronomical objects would be required to point at certain such objects through telescope.
  2. Minimum time shall be provided, based on which points are to be rewarded.
  3. Team with maximum score would be declared winner.

Into The Universe

This event consist of 3 rounds Spacewalk, Star Hunt and Messier marathon.

1st Round: Spacewalk

It will be an audio-visual quiz related to astronomy. Pictures and videos would be projected in front and participants have to answer the questions that follow.

2nd Round: Star Hunt

So are you one of those who sneak out of your rooms during night hours and wander long gazing at the sky, trying to build up your imagination. Well folks then this is a perfect game for you to play, a hunt for the stars. Participants need to set themselves on a voyage of different celestial bodies and conquer to best of their capabilities.

Problem Statement

Spacewalk:
  1. 20 audio/visual questions will be projected in front of the participants. They will have 1 minute to answer each question by writing it down on the given sheet.
  2. These questions will be objective or one word.
  3. Each question will carry 2 points.
  4. Only top 50% teams will go to the next round.
Star Hunt:
  1. Teams will be provided with certain token numbers and called upon on its basis.
  2. A star map and a laser pointer shall be provided to the team.
  3. Points will be given for locating the objects precisely only on first attempt.
  4. Based on the final scores, n-number of teams would qualify for the next level where n shall be dependant on participation number.
Messier Marathon:
  1. The teams which had qualified in pointing out the astronomical objects would be required to point at certain such objects through telescope.
  2. Minimum time shall be provided to the teams and the one who solves the maximum questions, earns maximum point.
  3. Team with maximum score would be declared winner.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Participants can form team of up to three.
  2. Using mobile phones is not allowed.
  3. Teams are suppose to restrain themselves from using any android application such as Google Sky Map or any other electronic media.
